Three types of payment processes at StorageHQ

Credit Card Payment Paying with your Ramp CC for business unit expenses.
  • Travel bookings
  • Office supplies & software subscriptions
  • General business unit purchases
  • Container Sales expenses
  • Multi-project costs
Project-Related Expenses tied to a specific client project or facility.
  • Third-party client builds
  • Leasing & acquisition costs
  • Facility operations
  • Active projects tracked in Smartsheets
Invoice & Vendor Payment Invoices, vendor payments, or unpaid bills sent to AP.
  • Vendor payments via Payables
  • US business expenses (not project-tied)
  • CAD business expenses (not project-tied)
  • Unpaid invoices submitted through Smartsheets

Procurement

When to use procurement

Procurement manages RFPs and POs through Smartsheets. Here's when to use each.

Request For Proposal
Smartsheets RFP Form
  • Items over $10,000
  • Going to market for the best price, vendor, or service
  • Ordering bulk product for multiple jobs
Purchase Order
Smartsheets PO Form
  • Frequently used items already sourced and approved
  • Ship to warehouse for bundled crate shipment later

When NOT to use procurement

  • Single item shipped direct to a location other than the warehouse
  • Urgent purchase with manager's approval to bypass procurement
Step-by-Step

How to use procurement

Detailed steps for the RFP and PO processes.

Request for Proposal Process (RFP)

Allow 2+ weeks lead time
  1. Fill out the Smartsheets RFP form with your scope and details
  2. Procurement follows up with questions to confirm requirements
  3. Procurement takes the RFP to market for bids
  4. Procurement sorts bids and presents the best options to you
  5. Contact your chosen vendor to discuss details and award the bid
  6. Work with Procurement to draft the contract, deliverables, and payment terms
  7. Procurement arranges with Finance to set up a USD vendor in QBO
  8. Procurement handles contract signatures and vendor onboarding
After onboarding: Once onboarded you will be the main vendor contact point for all invoices, questions etc. Procurement will no longer be in contact with the vendor. You own this Vendor now.

PO Process

Allow 4+ weeks for large orders
  1. Fill out the Smartsheets PO form (Instruct procurement what Business Unit this is for)
  2. Procurement will follow up to confirm the order details and timelines
  3. Procurement will have all items shipped/stored at our warehouse and ready to ship
  4. You will provide the location where the shipment will be delivered
  5. Procurement will have your order crated and delivered anywhere in the US within 5 business days
  6. You are responsible for all shipment details & logistics once it leaves the warehouse
